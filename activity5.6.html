Analysis

AOL

2002 was the last year with measurable AOL Browser usage.

Internet Explorer

Explorer usage has steadily decreased from 83% in 2002 to 7% in 2015.

Netscape

Similar to AOL, Netscape has not had measurable usage since 2004.

Mozzilla

Mozzilla saw a short life, beginning at 7% in 2003, and ending at 1% in 2007. The peak was 17% in 2004.

Opera

Opera, a web-based VPN, entered the market in 2003, at 2%, and has maintained 2% yearly 2003 - 2015.

Firefox

Firefox entered the market in 2005 at 24%, grew annually to 46% in 2009, and decreased annually to 22% in 2015.

Safari

Safari entered the market in 2008 at 3%, with 4% annually 2009 - 2015.

Chrome

Google Chrome entered the market in 2008 at 4%, and exhibited substantial growth annually to 65% in 2015.
